THE WORLD'S FINEST MILLS

A masterpiece begins with the thread. We source exclusively from the most prestigious textile houses in Italy and England.

UNCOMPROMISING QUALITY

At Newman Exclusive, we believe that the drape, breathability, and longevity of a suit are determined long before the first cut is made. Our library features over 5,000 fabric swatches, ranging from rugged English tweeds to ultra-fine Italian Super 180s wool.

OUR PARTNERS IN EXCELLENCE

Loro Piana

Renowned for the world’s finest cashmere and wool. Italian elegance at its absolute peak.

Vitale Barberis Canonico

With over 350 years of heritage, they provide the gold standard for classic business suiting.

Dormeuil

French luxury meets English manufacturing, known for innovative textures and luxury blends.

Holland & Sherry

The pride of Savile Row. Providing prestigious, durable fabrics since 1836.

The "Super" Numbers

Learn the difference between Super 100s and Super 180s. While higher numbers are finer and softer, lower numbers offer better durability for daily wear.

Weight & Seasonality

From 240g summer linens to 340g winter flannels, we guide you in selecting the correct weight for your climate and lifestyle.
