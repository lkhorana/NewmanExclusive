Weddings

The Wedding Timeline: The Perfect Fit

Your wedding day is likely the most photographed day of your life. While the focus is often on the bride's gown, the groom’s attire sets the sartorial standard. To ensure every stitch is perfect, we recommend beginning your journey with Newman Exclusive at least 8 to 10 weeks before your wedding date.

Phase 1: Week 1-2 (The Vision)

The process begins with a consultation that goes beyond simple measurements. We discuss the logistics: Are you hosting a sunset ceremony on a beach in Koh Samui, or a formal black-tie gala? During this phase, we explore thousands of swatches from the world’s finest mills to find the perfect weight and texture.

"A wedding suit is not just a garment; it is a future heirloom that carries the memory of your most important day."

Phase 2: Week 4-5 (The Basted Fitting)

This is where the magic begins. A "basted" fitting is a temporary version of your suit, held together by white basting thread. It allows our master tailors to analyze your unique posture and shoulder slope, ensuring the garment highlights your physique perfectly before the final seams are closed.

Phase 3: Final Delivery

When you walk out of our atelier with your finished garment, you aren't just carrying a suit—you are carrying the confidence that you are the best-dressed man in the room. By following this structured timeline, you ensure that your wedding attire is a masterpiece of personal style and technical precision.
