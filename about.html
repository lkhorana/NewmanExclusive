How It All Began

Attention to detail, a dream, and a sewing machine—these were the three pillars that brought Newman Exclusive to life. What started as a passion for the perfect silhouette has evolved into one of Bangkok’s most respected tailoring institutions.

For over three decades, we have served a global clientele, building a reputation not just for the suits we create, but for the relationships we forge. Our evolution from the Amari Watergate to our new flagship home at the InterContinental Bangkok marks a new chapter in our pursuit of luxury.
