Trousers & Fit

The Art of Customizing Your Trousers

Each individual has unique preferences when it comes to choosing the perfect fit of pants. Some may favor a snug fit, others may opt for a slim fit, while some might prefer a looser, more comfortable fit. Beyond the silhouette, what other customization options are available for trousers?

Front Aesthetic: Pleats vs. Flat

To begin with, we must decide whether we prefer a flat front or a pleated front. When it comes to pleated pants, it's important to choose the number of pleats that suits your preference—whether it's single pleated, double pleated, or more. Generally, the more pleats there are, the roomier the pants will be.

The Lower Silhouette

Having addressed the front appearance, we can now move on to the lower part of the pants. You have the option to choose between straight or rolled-up (cuffed) bottoms. This small detail can significantly shift the formality and weight of how the fabric drapes over your shoes.

The Waistband Statement

The waistband of pants offers a variety of customization options, allowing you to truly personalize the garment. At Newman Exclusive, we offer several styles:

  • • Straight-cut bands
  • • Extended bands (D-ring or Button)
  • • Traditional belt loops
  • • Beltless / Side-adjuster waistbands
  • • Gurkha pant style

Functional Details: Pocket Design

Another crucial aspect of customizing your pants is choosing the design for the front pockets. Depending on your daily needs and aesthetic preference, you can choose between straight side pockets, slanted side pockets, or traditional front pockets.

Having finalized the customization of your pants, we are excited to invite you to visit Newman Exclusive. Our team is here to help you design and craft your perfect fitting pants.
